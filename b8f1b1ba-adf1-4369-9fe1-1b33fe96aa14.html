Description

Get a taste of managing your own laundry store in this Demo version of Laundry Store Simulator! In this demo you will be able to play the opening sections of the game. There's no limit of how long you can play the demo, but available features and your progression are limited compared to the full game. Your progress and save data from the demo can not be transferred to the full version of the game.

Are you ready to own the world's best laundry store? Transform a small laundry shop into a laundry empire!

Discover the following features in Laundry Store Simulator:

Build a small laundry store into a thriving business empire! Upgrade your equipment, design and expand your store, hire staff, and more!

Upgrade your equipment to provide exceptional cleaning services! Invest in high-quality washing machines, dryers, ironing equipment, and even automated washing machines!

Create a stylish and inviting space to attract customers! Provide comfortable seating, warm decorations, and more funsies to welcome your customers!

Grow your laundry business by unlocking new areas and features, offering complimentary services to attract more customers!

Build a dedicated team to manage your laundry store efficiently! Hire qualified and experienced staff laundry attendants, such as, washers, dryers, and more!

Boost your profits by selling cleaning supplies and becoming a one-stop shop for laundry needs! Offer a variety of cleaning products, including detergents, fabric softeners, stain removers, and more!

Offer convenient home delivery to build a loyal customer base! Optimize delivery routes for efficiency and cost-effectiveness and provide excellent customer service during the delivery process!
